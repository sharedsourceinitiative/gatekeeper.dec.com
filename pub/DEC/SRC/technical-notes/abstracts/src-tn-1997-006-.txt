"The Operators of TLA+"
Leslie Lamport
Note #1997-006. April 12, 1997. 20 pages 

This document is an introduction to the syntax and semantics of the
operators of TLA+. It assumes that you are familiar with ordinary
mathematics (sets and functions) and are at least acquainted with TLA. It
should enable you to understand the expressions that appear in TLA+
specifications.
