"A Logical View of Composition"
Martin Abadi and Gordon D. Plotkin
May 1, 1992.  35 pages


Authors' abstract

We define two logics of safety specifications for reactive systems. 
The logics provide a setting for the study of composition  rules.
The two logics arise naturally from extant specification approaches; 
one of the logics is intuitionistic, while the other one is linear.
