SRC Research Report 119

How to Write a Long Formula

Leslie Lamport

December 25, 1993
6 pages

Standard mathematical notation works well for shortformulas, but not for the longer ones often written by computer scientists. Notations are proposed to make one or two-page formulas easier to read and reason about.
